Simple Dredark Tools

Thank you for installing!

  • The extension will not have any effect on Dredark pages loaded before the installation, so please make sure to refresh your previously loaded Dredark pages!

  • To configure the extension, you need to open its pop-up menu by clicking the extension icon (helm) in the toolbar at top right of your browser. If you don't see the icon, the extension might not be pinned to the toolbar. Click on the "Extensions" button (puzzle/box symbol) at top right of your browser and pin the extension.

  • In the pop-up menu you will find feature configurations that you may need to change frequently, and a gear button for the other features (you can also right click the extension icon then select Options).

  • If you encounter a problem or have a question, feel free to contact me on Discord.


Enjoy!
