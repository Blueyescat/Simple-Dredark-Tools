Simple Dredark Tools
Thank you for installing!

  • The extension will not have any effect on Dredark tabs loaded before the installation, so please make sure to refresh your previously loaded Dredark tabs!

  • To configure the extension, you need to open its pop-up menu by clicking the extension icon (helm) in the toolbar at the top right of your browser. If you don't see the icon, the extension might not be pinned to the toolbar. Click on the "Extensions" button (puzzle/box symbol) at the top right of your browser and pin the extension.

  • In the pop-up menu, you will find feature configurations that you may need to change frequently, and a gear button for other features (you can also right-click the extension icon and then select Options).

  • If you encounter a problem or have a question, feel free to contact me on Discord.

Enjoy!

You may also like DSA Tools, a website for various tools for the game (e.g. blueprint editing).
