Auto Setter
How it works?
If this is enabled, while pressing the hotkey and clicking a block in game will change its properties as defined here. Signs also support auto setting while placing them.

The auto setting can't work so fast, you shouldn't try to click fast, holding down left-click also works. You can't smoothly move while setting blocks.
